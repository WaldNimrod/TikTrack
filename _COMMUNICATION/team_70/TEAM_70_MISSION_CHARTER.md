---

## 1) Purpose

Team 70 is a dedicated non-execution unit focused on building a complete product/business knowledge package that supports:
- marketing homepage planning for non-registered users
- investor and partner presentation preparation
- strategic scope alignment between roadmap, legacy, SSOT, and live code

---

## 2) Reporting and Authority

- **Primary reporting line:** Architect + Team 90.
- **Not managed by Team 10** as a development execution team.
- **Team 10 interface:** receives finalized promotion-ready deltas for SSOT publication only.

---

## 3) Responsibilities (Mandatory)

1. Build a full map of:
   - interfaces/pages
   - features and user journeys
   - core processes and data dependencies
2. Produce a product/business narrative package with evidence.
3. Detect and document scope gaps, contradictions, and missing decisions.
4. Provide structured recommendations with ownership and priority.

---

## 4) Out of Scope

- No implementation tasks for UI/API/DB.
- No direct edits in `documentation/` SSOT files.
- No mandates to execution teams (20/30/40/50/60) without Architect/Team 90 approval.

---

## 5) Required Deliverables

| ID | Deliverable | Required Content |
|----|-------------|------------------|
| PI-001 | Product Scope Atlas | planned vs implemented pages, features, entities, dependencies |
| PI-002 | Business Narrative Pack | ICP, value narrative, pains/jobs, strategic differentiation |
| PI-003 | Marketing Input Pack | homepage messaging inputs, trust signals, CTA flow, proof assets needed |
| PI-004 | Investor/Partner Input Pack | business model assumptions, GTM hypotheses, integration value map |
| PI-005 | Gap Register | blockers, missing data, conflicting docs, decisions needed |

---

## 6) Operating Workflow

1. **Collect:** read SSOT + legacy + current code state.
2. **Map:** connect pages/features/processes with explicit evidence paths.
3. **Validate:** Team 90 performs consistency scan (no assumptions).
4. **Approve:** Architect reviews package direction.
5. **Promote:** Team 10 receives promotion-ready updates.

---

## 7) Acceptance Criteria

- Every claim includes at least one file-path evidence reference.
- No unresolved contradiction inside the final package.
- Gap register includes owner + severity + action + due-stage.
- Marketing and investor inputs are directly traceable to system capabilities.
- Final package approved by Team 90 pre-review before Architect handoff.

---

## 8) Coordination Rules

- Weekly checkpoint with Team 90.
- Escalate unresolved conflicts to Architect within 24h.
- Keep all draft work under `_COMMUNICATION/team_70/`.
