## 🔄 Invalidation Strategy

### **תבניות Invalidation:**
- `cash-flow-created` → invalidate `account-activity-*`, `accounts-data`
- `cash-flow-updated` → invalidate `account-activity-*`, `accounts-data`
- `cash-flow-deleted` → invalidate `account-activity-*`, `accounts-data`
- `execution-created` → invalidate `account-activity-*`, `accounts-data`
- `execution-updated` → invalidate `account-activity-*`, `accounts-data`
- `execution-deleted` → invalidate `account-activity-*`, `accounts-data`

### **TTL מומלץ:**
- `account-activity-*`: 60 שניות
- `accounts-data`: 120 שניות (כולל רשימת כל החשבונות)
